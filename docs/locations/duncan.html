Duncan Tree Service

Duncan is the county seat of Stephens County and one of the largest communities in south-central Oklahoma, with a population of roughly 22,000 residents. Located about 35 miles east of Lawton along Highway 7, Duncan features established neighborhoods lined with mature shade trees, pecan groves, and a mix of native hardwoods that define the character of the community. Lawton Tree Pros is proud to extend our professional tree care services to Duncan homeowners and commercial property owners who depend on healthy, well-maintained trees.

Duncan's tree-lined streets and older residential areas are home to large post oaks, elms, pecans, and redbuds that have grown for decades. While these mature trees add beauty and value to properties, they also require regular professional attention to stay safe and healthy. Oklahoma's severe weather, including ice storms, high winds, and summer drought, takes a toll on aging trees. Without proper care, weakened limbs and diseased trunks can become serious hazards. That is where our experienced crew comes in.

Tree Services in Duncan

Lawton Tree Pros provides Duncan residents with the full range of professional tree care. Every service is performed by trained professionals using proper equipment and strict safety procedures.

  • Tree Removal — Safe removal of dead, hazardous, or unwanted trees of any size, with complete debris cleanup.
  • Tree Trimming & Pruning — Expert shaping, canopy thinning, and deadwood removal to promote health and curb appeal.
  • Stump Grinding — Eliminate unsightly stumps below grade to reclaim yard space and prevent regrowth.
  • Emergency Tree Service — 24/7 storm damage response when fallen trees threaten your home, vehicle, or power lines.
  • Tree Health Assessment — Professional diagnosis of disease, pest infestations, and structural weaknesses before problems escalate.
  • Lot Clearing — Complete land clearing for new construction, fencing, or property improvement projects.
