Chickasha Tree Service

Chickasha is the county seat of Grady County and one of central Oklahoma's most established communities, with a population of around 16,000 residents. Located about 45 miles northeast of Lawton along US-62, Chickasha blends small-city amenities with tree-lined neighborhoods that have been growing for over a century. Comanche Tree Experts is proud to extend our professional tree care services to Chickasha homeowners, businesses, and property managers who need reliable, licensed tree work done right.

Tree Services in Chickasha

Chickasha's older neighborhoods feature large, mature shade trees that add character and value to residential properties. Many homes near the University of Science and Arts of Oklahoma campus and along the historic downtown corridors sit beneath towering oaks, elms, and pecans that have been growing for decades. These mature trees require regular professional attention to stay healthy and safe. Without proper trimming, aging limbs can become liabilities during Oklahoma's severe storm season, and unchecked disease can spread from one tree to the next.

We provide the full range of tree care services to Chickasha residents. Our tree removal service safely takes down hazardous, dead, or unwanted trees of any size, including large hardwoods in tight residential lots. Professional tree trimming and pruning keeps canopies healthy, improves sunlight penetration, and reduces wind resistance before storm season. Stump grinding eliminates unsightly stumps left behind after removal, restoring your yard and preventing regrowth. Our emergency tree service is available 24/7 for storm damage and fallen trees that threaten structures, vehicles, or power lines. We also offer tree health assessments to diagnose disease, insect damage, or structural weakness, and complete lot clearing for new construction, development, or overgrown property cleanup.

Serving Chickasha and Grady County

Although Chickasha is farther from our Lawton base than some of our other service areas, we make the drive regularly because Chickasha residents deserve access to quality, professional tree care. We serve all of Grady County and coordinate scheduling to ensure Chickasha customers receive prompt, efficient service. Whether you own a home in one of Chickasha's established neighborhoods, manage rental properties near the university, or need commercial tree work downtown, our licensed and insured crew brings the same professional standards to every job.

We also serve Lawton, Duncan, Anadarko, and all communities in between. If you need tree service in Chickasha or anywhere in Grady County, call us at (580) 781-1781 for a free estimate. Every job includes full cleanup, and we stand behind our work with a commitment to your complete satisfaction.
