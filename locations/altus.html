Altus Tree Service

Situated about 60 miles southwest of Lawton in Jackson County, Altus is a proud community of roughly 18,000 residents anchored by Altus Air Force Base. Comanche Tree Experts brings dependable, professional tree care to Altus homeowners, military families stationed at the base, and commercial property owners throughout the area. From established neighborhoods near downtown to housing around the base and rural properties on the outskirts of town, our experienced crew delivers quality tree work tailored to the needs of southwest Oklahoma landscapes.

Tree Services in Altus

The southwest Oklahoma climate creates specific challenges for trees in the Altus area. Hot, dry summers stress root systems and foliage, while strong plains winds can snap weakened branches or topple compromised trees entirely. Severe spring thunderstorms and occasional winter ice events add further risk. Common tree species found on Altus properties include mesquite, red cedar, hackberry, American elm, bur oak, and pecan trees, all of which require knowledgeable care to stay healthy and structurally sound in this environment.

We provide Altus and Jackson County residents with six core tree care services:

  • Tree Removal -- safe removal of dead, diseased, storm-damaged, or unwanted trees of any size
  • Tree Trimming -- expert pruning to improve health, shape, clearance, and wind resistance
  • Stump Grinding -- complete stump elimination to reclaim yard space and prevent regrowth
  • Emergency Tree Service -- 24/7 rapid response for storm damage and hazardous tree situations
  • Tree Health Assessment -- professional evaluation to identify disease, pest damage, and structural concerns
  • Lot Clearing -- full land clearing for construction, development, and property improvement projects

Military families at Altus AFB often need reliable tree service on tight timelines, whether preparing a property before a PCS move, dealing with unexpected storm damage, or maintaining trees around base-area housing. We work efficiently and respect your schedule, delivering prompt service with thorough cleanup on every job.
