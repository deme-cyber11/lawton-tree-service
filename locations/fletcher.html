Fletcher Tree Service

Fletcher is a small, close-knit community of about 1,100 residents in northern Comanche County, just a short drive north of Lawton along US-277. With its quiet neighborhoods, spacious rural properties, and surrounding farmland lined with large shade trees, Fletcher has a character that sets it apart. Comanche Tree Experts is proud to bring professional tree care to every homeowner and landowner in Fletcher and the surrounding countryside.

Many properties in and around Fletcher sit on generous lots where mature oaks, elms, and cottonwoods have grown for decades. These trees provide welcome shade during Oklahoma's brutal summers, but they also demand regular attention. Severe spring thunderstorms, winter ice accumulation, and prolonged drought can weaken branches, stress root systems, and leave dead limbs hanging overhead. Our experienced crew understands these challenges and has the equipment to handle trees of any size safely and efficiently on both residential lots and larger rural acreage.
